About SAFA

Safa is your personal assistant when it comes to understanding what your customers are really saying. Whether it's praise, complaints, or helpful suggestions, Safa carefully goes through every review to give you a clear picture of how people feel about your product or service. It not only captures the mood of your customers but also highlights common themes and offers thoughtful ideas to help you improve. Safa turns scattered feedback into meaningful insightsâ€”so you can focus more on action, and less on sorting through words.

Whether you're running a small business or a growing brand, Safa helps you stay connected with your customers' voices in a simple, human way.

The name Safa is inspired by the full form of the projectâ€”Customer Feedback Analyser (CFA). Rearranged and softened into Safa, the name carries a calm and friendly tone, much like the experience it offers. Just as the name suggests, Safa is here to bring clarity, simplicity, and a touch of ease to what often feels like a messy pile of feedback. This project is built using cutting-edge technologies like:

  • Groq API - Powering high-speed AI responses using the Mistral model
  • LangChain - Orchestrating smooth flow and management of feedback tasks
  • Google Gemini - Bringing reviews to life through an interactive chatbot experience
  • Flask - Serving the backend logic and routes efficiently
  • HTML, CSS & JavaScript - Creating a clean and user-friendly frontend
