About Safa

Safa is your personal assistant when it comes to understanding what your customers are really saying. Whether it's praise, complaints, or helpful suggestions, Safa carefully goes through every review to give you a clear picture of how people feel about your product or service. It not only captures the mood of your customers but also highlights common themes and offers thoughtful ideas to help you improve. Safa turns scattered feedback into meaningful insights—so you can focus more on action, and less on sorting through words.

Whether you're running a small business or a growing brand, Safa helps you stay connected with your customers' voices in a simple, human way.

Why the name "Safa"?
The name Safa comes from the project's full name—Customer Feedback Analyser (CFA). By rearranging the letters and softening the tone, the name Safa was born. It has a calm, friendly feel that reflects what the tool aims to do: make sense of chaotic feedback and turn it into something meaningful. It's not just a name—it's the personality of the experience. Simple, warm, and purposeful.

This project is built using cutting-edge technologies like:

  • Groq API - Powering high-speed AI responses using the Mistral model
  • LangChain - Orchestrating smooth flow and management of feedback tasks
  • Google Gemini - Bringing reviews to life through an interactive chatbot experience
  • Flask - Serving the backend logic and routes efficiently
  • HTML, CSS & JavaScript - Creating a clean and user-friendly frontend
